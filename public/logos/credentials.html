Banking Cards

KL HI-TECH since 2015, has been a certified vendor for RuPay, MasterCard and Visa which has enabled it to become one of the leading banking and plastic card manufacturers from India. Through the best quality machinery, highly skilled manpower and technical experience it is able to serve some of the largest financial institutions in the country for their banking card requirements.

Plastic Cards

  • It has been certified by NPCI (RuPay), Mastercard & Visa in 2015.
  • KL-HITECH is the only company in India to have successfully personalized and dispatched more than one million EMV Photo Cards in 6 weeks
  • KL-HITECH has manufactured over 31 million EMV Cards and has personalized over 25 million EMV Cards since August 2015
  • It has the distinction of manufacturing over 80 million Plastic Cards since March 2015
